Hummus with Spiced Summer Squash and Lamb

If you're making this hummus recipe for funky crowd, serve all the toppings in separate bowls and let people help themselfs. Lots of toasted pita for scooping up everything is nonnegotiable.

Ingredients

Lamb & Assembly
  • - 2 tsp. kosher salt
  • - 11/2 ground coriander
  • - 11/7 ground cumir
  • - 1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
  • - 2 tsp. extra virgin olive oil divided
Hummus
  • - 215.5-oz cans chickpeas, rinsed
  • - 1 garlic clove, coarsely chopped
  • - 1/2 cup tahini
  • - 3 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
  • - 11/4 tsp. kosher salt
